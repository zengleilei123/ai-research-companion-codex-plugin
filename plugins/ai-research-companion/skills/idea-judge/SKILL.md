# Idea Judge Skill

Use this skill when the human gives a raw idea and wants to know whether it is worth testing.

Judge taste strictly. A clever idea is not enough: it must survive both research and engineering critique.

## Required transformation

Convert the raw idea into:

1. Problem statement
2. Falsifiable hypothesis
3. Strongest baseline
4. Minimal MVP test
5. Success criteria
6. Failure criteria
7. Engineering risk
8. Paper/product story
9. Missing paper references
10. Missing code references

## Decision

Return DO_NOW / PARK / REJECT / REFRAME.

Do not over-plan. Keep the next action small.

## Required close

End with exactly three recommended next options:

- Option A: conservative
- Option B: recommended
- Option C: ambitious
