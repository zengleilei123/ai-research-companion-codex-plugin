# Weekly Review Skill

Use this skill to help the human reflect on long-cycle AI research.

## Inputs

- journal/daily
- experiments/*/results.md
- knowledge/insights
- knowledge/pitfalls
- decisions

## Output

- what worked
- what failed
- repeated patterns
- insights worth preserving
- directions to continue
- directions to pause or kill
- top 1-3 next actions

Do not create a huge plan. Prioritize focus.
